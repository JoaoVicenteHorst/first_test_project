THE PROBLEM:
Your frontend can't talk to your backend because FRONTEND_URL isn't set!

THE FIX:
Go to Render Dashboard and set environment variables.

================================================================================
STEP 1: CHECK BACKEND LOGS
================================================================================

1. Go to: https://dashboard.render.com
2. Click your BACKEND service
3. Click "Logs" tab (left sidebar)
4. Look for this when it starts:

   ‚ö†Ô∏è  WARNING: No production origins configured!
   ‚ö†Ô∏è  Set FRONTEND_URL environment variable

If you see this warning ‚Üí FRONTEND_URL is missing!

================================================================================
STEP 2: SET FRONTEND_URL
================================================================================

1. Stay in your BACKEND service
2. Click "Environment" tab (left sidebar)
3. Look for FRONTEND_URL

   NOT THERE?
   ‚Üí Click "Add Environment Variable"
   ‚Üí Key: FRONTEND_URL
   ‚Üí Value: https://your-frontend-name.onrender.com
   ‚Üí Click "Save Changes"

   ALREADY THERE?
   ‚Üí Click the pencil icon to edit
   ‚Üí Make sure it EXACTLY matches your frontend URL
   ‚Üí No typos, no trailing slash
   ‚Üí Click "Save Changes"

4. Backend will automatically redeploy (wait 1-2 min)

================================================================================
STEP 3: VERIFY IT WORKS
================================================================================

1. Backend logs should now show:

   ‚úÖ https://your-frontend.onrender.com (FRONTEND_URL)

2. Visit your frontend
3. Try to login
4. Should work! ‚úÖ

================================================================================

‚ùå WRONG:  FRONTEND_URL=http://my-app.onrender.com
‚úÖ RIGHT:  FRONTEND_URL=https://my-app.onrender.com
